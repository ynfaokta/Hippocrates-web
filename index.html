About Hippocrates

Hippocrates is an event that will be held by the Faculty of Medicine, Trisakti University, organized by students from the Faculty of Medicine, Trisakti University as a form of practicing the Tridharma of higher education, especially in terms of Education and Community Service.

This year, we have the theme “Daily Exercise” which was made in a Seminar with the title “Youthful Vitality: Nourishing the Future through Nutrition and Exercise”We have a daily theme Exercise is the right step to educate the public about the importance of exercise and maintaining nutritional balance in the body by maintaining diet and nutrition intake.
